6. Cross-Border Transfers of Personal Data

Some of the third-party services listed in Section 5 — including Google LLC (Google Analytics 4, reCAPTCHA, Google Fonts) and Microsoft Corporation (Microsoft Clarity) — are based in the United States and process data on servers located outside Malaysia.

These transfers occur in the following circumstances:

  • Analytics and session recording (Google Analytics 4, Microsoft Clarity): These services are only loaded after you have given your consent via the cookie consent banner on this website. Your consent to the loading of these cookies constitutes your consent to the associated transfer of data to Google's and Microsoft's servers in the United States, as required under Section 129(3) of the Personal Data Protection Act 2010. You may withdraw this consent at any time as described in Section 5 of the Cookie Notice.
  • reCAPTCHA (Google LLC): When you interact with the contact form, data is transmitted to Google's servers for spam prevention purposes. This processing is described in Section 5 above and in the Cookie Notice. By submitting the contact form, you acknowledge this processing.
  • Google Fonts: Each page load transmits your IP address and browser information to Google's servers as part of the standard font delivery request. This is a technical necessity of using Google Fonts.

Google LLC and Microsoft Corporation maintain data processing agreements with their customers and implement standard contractual protections for international data transfers. For further information on how these parties protect transferred data, please refer to their respective privacy policies linked in Section 5.

8. Your Rights Under the PDPA

Under the Personal Data Protection Act 2010, you have the following rights in respect of your personal data held by Alphamatic:

8.1 Right of Access

You may request access to the personal data we hold about you. We will respond within a reasonable time and in accordance with our obligations under PDPA s.12.

8.2 Right to Correct

You may request that we correct any personal data we hold that is inaccurate, incomplete, misleading, or not up to date, in accordance with PDPA s.34.

8.3 Right to Withdraw Consent

Where processing is based on your consent (e.g. for analytics cookies or marketing messages), you may withdraw your consent at any time. Withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing before the withdrawal.

8.4 Right to Limit Processing

In limited circumstances, you may request that we cease or limit processing of your personal data.

8.5 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 9. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.
